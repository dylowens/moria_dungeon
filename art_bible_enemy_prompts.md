# Enemy Art Bible

This game's enemy pipeline uses two prompts per enemy:

1. A character creation prompt
2. A transparent 8-direction asset prompt

The goal is consistent dark-fantasy realism that still reads clearly in gameplay.

## Shared Rules

- Ancient subterranean fantasy realism
- Believable anatomy and materials
- Dark-fantasy mood, grounded rather than stylized
- Strong silhouette readability
- No cartoon, anime, icon, chibi, or flat vector styling
- Final runtime sheets should be transparent and solid, with no background effects outside the body silhouette
