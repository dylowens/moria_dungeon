# HUB Backdrop Prompt

Use this for the HUB background image.

## Goal

Create a new HUB backdrop in the same grounded dark-fantasy realism as the enemy art bible, but centered on a monumental gate inspired by the Mines of Moria.

## Primary Prompt

```text
Ancient subterranean fantasy realism, grounded dark-fantasy environment concept art, monumental hidden gate inspired by the Mines of Moria as the main HUB backdrop, colossal stone doors set into a mountain-like underground wall, weathered dwarven-style architecture, carved runes, broken stairs, ancient pillars, worn stone, black iron details, faint cold mist near the ground, dim shafts of pale light, subtle teal-gray and ember-gold lighting accents, believable realistic materials, cinematic but restrained lighting, moody underground atmosphere, abandoned sacred threshold, epic scale, centered composition, symmetrical read, strong focal point on the gate, wide open foreground plaza for UI readability, clear negative space in the center and lower middle, no characters, no enemies, no text, no logo, no interface, no heavy fog obscuring the gate, highly detailed environment concept art, photoreal fantasy matte-painting quality, not cartoon, not anime, not comic, not flat vector, not cel shaded, not cute, not chibi, not oversaturated
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
characters, people, creature, monster, hero, crowd, weapons in foreground, extreme bloom, high saturation, neon colors, cartoon, anime, comic, stylized mobile game art, flat vector, cel shading, low detail, blurry, noisy image, text, logo, watermark, frame, border, UI, excessive fog, black crush, overly bright fire, lava, modern architecture
```

## Output Guidance

- Landscape image
- Target aspect ratio: `1040x760`
- Safe upscale options: `2080x1520` or `3120x2280`
- Keep the gate centered
- Keep the lower-middle area readable and less busy so overlay/menu elements can sit on top
- Favor stone, iron, dust, and dim mystical light over action or spectacle

## Variation Note

If your generator supports style strength or prompt weight, keep it realistic and restrained. This should feel like a solemn ancient threshold, not a boss-fight splash screen.
